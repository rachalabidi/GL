TheSoftware Engineering license offers training in software engineering in order to:
Integration into the professional world The pursuit of studies: masters, engineering schools, teacher training.
The “Software Engineering” license has six semesters, including :
The first four semesters are semesters common to all degrees in the “IT” field.
The fifth and sixth semesters (S5 and S6) are specific semesters of the specialty.
The S5 semester focuses on theoretical and practical aspects related to software engineering. It includes lessons such as: software design, software development, advanced database techniques, software quality (tools and methods), advanced web technologies and software project management.
The objective of the semester (S6) is to put into practice the various courses (theoretical, technical, organisational and human aspects…) of the S5 on a large-scale IT project (in the form of a workshop lasting about 12 weeks). In conjunction with this project, other practical lessons are planned such as network administration.
